The science

Ingredients that actually work

Salicylic Acid (BHA)
Oil-soluble exfoliant that penetrates deep into pores to dissolve sebum and dead skin. Clinically proven for acne reduction in melanin-rich skin.
Niacinamide (Vitamin B3)
Regulates sebum production, minimises pore appearance, and inhibits melanin transfer — addressing hyperpigmentation without bleaching agents.
Hydrocolloid technology
Medical-grade wound healing material that creates a moist environment, absorbs pus and sebum from spots, and prevents scarring and picking.
Zinc PCA
Regulates oil production and has mild antibacterial properties — works synergistically with niacinamide to control shine in humid climates.
